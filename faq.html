Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Q: I got an offer in the mail, how do I accept the offer?

A: Please mail back the filled offer with your information and signed to 111 Middlesex Turnpike #1110, Burlington, MA 01803. You can also scan the signed offer and Email it back to us for faster processing.

Q: How does the selling process work with ezlandbuyer.com?

A: Selling your land to ezlandbuyer.com is simple. Just fill out our online form with details about your property, receive a cash offer, accept the offer, and we'll take care of the rest.

Q: Do I need to make any repairs or improvements to my land before selling it?

A: No! We buy land in AS-IS condition, so you don't need to worry about making any repairs or improvements. We'll take your land exactly as it is.

Q: How long does it take to receive an offer from ezlandbuyer.com?

A: We strive to provide offers as quickly as possible. In most cases, you can expect to receive an offer within 24-48 hours of submitting your property information.

Q: Will I incur any fees or costs when selling my land to ezlandbuyer.com?

A: No! There are no hidden fees or costs when selling your land to us. We cover all closing costs and fees associated with the transaction.

Q: What types of land does ezlandbuyer.com purchase?

A: We purchase all types of land, including rural plots, vacant lots, acreage, and more. No matter the size or location, we're interested in buying your land.

Q: What is the expected timeframe for closing?

A: We aim for swift and efficient transactions. Generally, we strive to complete the closing process in less than 30 days.

Q: Could you please explain the due diligence period?

A: The due diligence period is a crucial phase during which our team conducts comprehensive research on the land. This includes examining zoning restrictions, land usage regulations, conducting surveys, performing percolation tests, verifying ownership, and ensuring all necessary steps are taken to confirm the land's suitability for purchase.
